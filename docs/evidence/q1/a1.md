# Q1 — Use case design

## Context

This use case is about an IT, cloud, and AI transformation consulting company specialised in AWS and GCP. Daveo is a French company that is part of a larger group called Magellan Partners. It has around 300 employees across five French cities and works with large clients such as TotalEnergies, ENGIE, and Decathlon.

This case is personal because I have worked there and created an internal AI platform with multiple chatbots for the company. Today, consultants, business managers, and HR teams lose a lot of time on repetitive tasks where AI could help them optimise their work, such as:

Analysing client documents
Responding to invitations to tender
Extracting questions from PDF or Word files
Matching consultant CVs to client requirements
Transforming messy data into structured tables or JSON
Digitising information from images, such as Post-it notes
Switching between different AI tools such as Claude, Gemini, AWS Bedrock, GCP tools, and manual files
Turning written input into an Excel file

However, a consulting company deals with sensitive data such as internal documents, CVs, client files, and cloud tools. Therefore, using a generic chatbot could create security, confidentiality, and compliance issues. For this reason, the proposed solution is an internal AI portal for employees only. This portal would centralise multiple AI applications into one secure interface and optimise time for consultants, marketing teams, and ESG teams by automating the tasks they usually handle.

## Personas

Consultant/Business Manager

Before automating the process, they receive a tender from a potential client or current one, usually not even one but multiples. They need to read and understand all the requirements as well as finding a potential consultant match which takes hours of going through CVs in order to match the proper consultant to the client’s need and once that is done, they need to write a first proposal for the client which has to be thorough. Responses need to be accurate and based on internal material, not hallucinated. The AI portal can go through all the CVs, extract the requirements and match them properly to suggest the best profiles

Marketing/Internal com Team

Before automating the process, they need to make sure the AI portal is usable, visually aligned with Daveo’s brand, and presentable internally or externally, to use as a use case for potential clients. Even with a great technical team, usually these kinds of tools are developer oriented and not aesthetically pleasing. They have to manage the needs of every department. LobeChat provides a user-friendly front end where tools are accessible through a single interface, while the backend can still connect to specialized AI and data tools.

ESG/Internal Project team

Before automating the process, the ESG team spent hours doing repetitive manual copy and paste. They would have a document with questions and relevant information and would have to write those in different columns of an Excel, as well as a last column for an answer they have to provide. They need something structured and reusable. The portal can extract questions from documents, generate answers, and output an Excel file automatically. This connects directly to the project you described in your internship report.

## Journey

Step 1

The first step is that the users log into the AI Portal, so consultants/marketing/ESG, etc. Here we will take only the business Manager/consultant’s journey as it's the most relevant. Once logged in, they can select which assistant they want to use. So let's say they just received a new tender from a client; then the ‘Tender Response Assistant’ and Lobechat act as the front-end interface using a reasoning model such as Claude or GPT.

Step 2

The Second Step of the journey is to upload the client's documents, so sensitive data, giving all the requirements needed for the position. Then the system extracts all these informations and give them to the business manager, such as the client's needs, the technical requirements needed by the consultant (senior/junior), and deadlines. Here, the MCP tools used are a PDF reader, document parser and OCR tool if the document was on paper and then scanned. Usually, the model here is document extraction or a summarisation model like Claude/GPT.

Step 3

Step 3 of the journey consists of the business manager searching through the company’s internal documents to look for similar proposals, consultants' CVs, key expertise needed, previous cases with that company and how it went. At this stage, the MCP tools are more internal document search, CV database Search, cloud storage and vector database/RAG tools. Moreover, the models are embedded for search and a reasoning model to rank consultants

Step 4

Step 4 of this journey is to generate a first draft proposal for the client. It can include any of this information: summary of the client need, proposed approach, relevant Daveo expertise, suggested consultant profiles, project timeline, risks and recommendations. At this stage, the MCP tools are more of a template tool, document generation tool or a company guide. The usual model is more towards long form writing.

Step 5

Step 5 of the Business Manager/Consultant journey is to ask follow-up questions to the AI assistant to polish it and make sure it meets the company’s standards. The final output can be exported to Word, PDF, or Excel. The MCP tools are usually excel generation/word exporter/table generator.

## Why this stack vs ChatGPT/Copilot

There are multiple reasons why this stack is better suited than ChatGPT/Copilot in this use case. Indeed, even though ChatGPT can summarize/write text, it does not automatically have access to Daveo’s database of CVs, past proposals, client documents and sensitive data, whereas LobeChat + MCP tools can connect the model directly to internal databases or tools needed. Moreover, in the case of Daveo, they need more than just a chatbot to help optimise time; they need to analyse PDFs, images, and data, and it needs ot be able to generate content such as Excel, PDF, DOCX, JSON and to match CVs properly. ChatGPT wouldn’t be able to help the workflow as its general purposed. Indeed, Daveo is also in search of flexibility and not use only one AI environment but multiple depending on the situation at hand; Claude, Gemini, AWS Bedrock, GCP, LangChain, and Streamlit during my internship. The stack can also choose the best option for them, knowing the best attributes of each. Finally, consulting companies handle very confidential data regarding their clients and cannot risk breaking that trust by using a general model that could leak the info to the internet. With an internal portal, they gain better control by managing authentication (only company emails are allowed), access rights and token usage.
